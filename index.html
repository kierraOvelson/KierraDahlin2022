About Me

Artistic and communication skills have always come naturally for me. The eagerness and desire to create, whether illustrations or building 3D models by hand was something that was never really taught, but was encouraged my entire life. Receiving training at NAIT in the Digital Media and IT program harnessed those natural abilities, while giving guidance in basic design and coding practices. Though at first a learning curve, through hardwork and determination to do well, this course ultimately helped me grow as a designer, coder, and a communicator.
